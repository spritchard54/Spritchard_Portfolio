About Me

I've worn many different hats throughout my professional career. After earning a B.S. in Communications Media from Indiana University of Pennsylvania I began my professional career as a photojournalist at WBRE-TV in Wilkes-Barre Pennsylvania. After nearly five years in that field, I felt like it was time to get out of the terrible weather and away from the low pay and I made the jump to work in media sales.

Not long after I caught the technology bug and began working in consulting as an Organizational Change Management Consultant. I learned a ton during that time about business, technology, and consulting. The focus was not on technology itself though. It was on people, processes, and technology. I currently work as a Customer Success Manager for a software company, but the role is still primarily account management.

I've always enjoyed building things. There is something satisfying about coding. Often, you're starting from scratch. Slowly adding in content and functionality. It's a hobby at the moment. Maybe more than a hobby down the road. Stay tuned.
